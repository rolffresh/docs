---
title: "Connecting Linear"
description: "LinCal does not need a separate account. You log in with your Linear account"
---

Just sign in with your Linear account. LinCal will automatically connect your workspace so you can start planning issues in calendar view.

## Connect your account

![Login screen](/images/getting-started/login-with-linear-account.png)


1. Browse to the log in page /login
2. Click **Sign in with Linear**.
3. Complete the Linear sign-in flow.
4. You will get redirected to LinCal and can start using your calendar.

## Troubleshooting

![Connect Linear screen](/images/getting-started/no-access-admin-message.png)

If you see a **"full team member or admin must install this app"** message, you are either a guest to a Linear workspace or an admin must approve it.
Contact your Linear workspace admin to grant access.
