---
title: "Connecting Linear"
description: "Connect your Linear workspace to LinCal to start syncing issues."
---

Connecting Linear is simple: just sign in with your Linear account. LinCal will automatically connect your workspace so you can start planning issues in calendar view.

## Connect your account

1. Open LinCal.
2. Click **Sign in with Linear**.
3. Complete the Linear sign-in flow.
4. Return to LinCal and start using your calendar.

## Troubleshooting

If you see a **"not able to install"** message, contact your Linear workspace admin to grant access.
