# Plans & billing

LinCal subscriptions are billed **per Linear workspace**. One subscription covers your whole workspace at a **flat rate** — not per-seat.

You’ll subscribe or manage payment inside LinCal.

---

## Overview

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Billed** | Per LinCal / Linear workspace |
| **Seat model** | Flat workspace price (not priced per member) |
| **Payment** | In-app checkout and billing portal |
| **Who can pay** | The workspace **billing owner** (see below) |

---

## Who needs a LinCal subscription?

Your workspace needs an active LinCal subscription when **all** of the following apply:

1. **Your workspace is on a paid Linear plan**
2. **At least two people use LinCal for that workspace**

If those conditions are **not** both met, you can use LinCal for that workspace **without** a subscription.

---

## Trial

The first time your workspace becomes subject to the rules above, LinCal starts a **trial** (by default **7 days** from when the trial is recorded for that workspace).

During the trial you have full access. When the trial ends without an active subscription, access to the calendar is blocked until the workspace is upgraded.

## Team plan & pricing

![Lin Cal Upgrade Modal](/images/using-lincal/LinCal-Upgrade-Modal.png)

The paid offering is the **Workspace** plan: one price for the **entire workspace**.

- **Monthly** and **annual** billing intervals are available; pick the interval in the upgrade flow.
- **Authoritative amounts** (including tax and currency as applicable) are always shown **at checkout** when you subscribe in the app.

If you need a quote for procurement, use the totals shown in checkout or ask [Support](/support) (or your usual LinCal contact).

---

## How to upgrade

![Lin Cal Upgrade Button](/images/using-lincal/LinCal-Upgrade-Button.png)

1. Sign in to LinCal with your Linear account.
2. When your workspace requires a subscription, follow the in-app **upgrade** flow.
3. Complete payment on the checkout page. After a successful purchase, your workspace should have access again (you may need to refresh or return from checkout as prompted).

Guests on a workspace cannot complete checkout; a full workspace member must subscribe (see **Billing owner**).

---

## Billing owner

Payments and many billing-management actions are tied to a single **billing owner** for the workspace:

- The first user doing the upgrade is turned into the billing owner
- The **billing owner** can **Manage billing** via a link in the user menu.
- **Other workspace members** should ask the billing owner to subscribe or update payment.

---

## Managing your subscription

After you’re subscribed:

![Manage billing](/images/using-lincal/LinCal-ManageBillingLink.png)

- Use **Manage billing** from the account menu in LinCal to open the **customer portal**.
- There you can typically update payment details, view invoices, or cancel the subscription.

---

## Cancellation

You can cancel from the billing portal. Your workspace **keeps access until the end of the current paid period**. After that, if a subscription is still required for your workspace (paid Linear \+ enough members), you’ll need to subscribe again to restore access.

---

## Past-due payments

If a renewal fails, your subscription may be marked **past due**. LinCal allows a **grace period** so you can update payment before access is interrupted. If payment isn’t fixed in time, access can be blocked until billing is successful.

If you’re blocked, use **Manage billing** (if you’re the billing owner) or ask the billing owner to update payment.
