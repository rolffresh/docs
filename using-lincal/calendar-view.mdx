title: "Calendar View"
description: "Understand how to navigate and use LinCal's calendar view."
---

LinCal shows your Linear issues by due date in a weekly calendar, so you can quickly see what needs attention.

## Calendar layout

![LinCal calendar view screenshot](/images/using-lincal/LinCal-CalendarOverview.png)


- **Top bar**: Filter, Display options, Sync, Today, Share, and account menu.
- **Calendar grid**: Days are shown Monday to Sunday (or Monday to Friday if weekends are hidden).
- **Task cards**: Each day shows up to 4 issues directly.
- **Overflow**: If a day has more than 4 issues, use **x more** to open the full list.
- **No due date bar**: A bottom section shows issues without a due date.

## Navigate the view

- Scroll to move through weeks.
- Use **Today** to jump back to the current week.
- Watch the month label in the header as you move through the calendar.

## Open and inspect issues

- Click an issue card to open it in Linear.
- Card details can include status, labels, project, and assignee (depending on Display settings).
- Overdue issues are marked on the card.
