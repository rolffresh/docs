## Move a task to another day

![Drag and drop screenshot](/images/using-lincal/LinCal-DragDrop.png)


1. Click and drag a task card.
2. Drop it on a different day cell in the calendar.
3. LinCal updates the due date immediately in Linear.

### Notes

- Only the due date changes.
- Day cells highlight when they are valid drop targets.

## Remove a due date

1. Drag a task card to the bottom bar: **Tasks without a due date**.
2. Drop it there.
3. The task is updated to have no due date.

### Notes

- Use this when a task should stay unscheduled.
- You can expand the bottom section to review all unscheduled tasks.

## Tips

- Use **Today** to quickly return to the current week before dropping.
- Overdue tasks can be rescheduled quickly with drag and drop.
